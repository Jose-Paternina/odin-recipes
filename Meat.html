Recipe For Meat On A Stick

The Only and best way to eat meat!

The favorite dish of the future pirate king!

Ingredients

  • Meat
  • Assorted spices
  • Assorted herbs
  • Stick

Instructions

  1. Make chicken drumstick tulips. Use kitchen scissors to cut the meat loose from the handle end of the drumstick (A). Roll the meat down the bone until it is fully inside-out at the end.
  2. Soak the breadcrumbs in milk. Knead mix (a.) in a bowl, then add breadcrumbs and knead again.
  3. Fold the meaty end of the drumstick around a hard-boiled egg (B). If the meat doesnt cover well enough, add cuts to loosen it up. Oil hands lightly and cover drumstick and egg with step 2 breadcrumb mixture (C).
  4. Bake at 400F (200C) for 15 to 20 minutes, watching carefully.
