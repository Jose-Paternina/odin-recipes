Recipe For Oden Noodles

Oden wouldn't be oden if it wasn't boiled!

The famouse Dish from the Shogun of wano himself!

Ingredients

  • Noodles
  • Assorted Fish Meat
  • Assorted Vegtables
  • Water
  • soy sauce

Instructions

  1. Lightly wipe kombu with a damp cloth on both sides. Bring water to a boil in a large pot then turn off the heat and add kombu pieces. Cover with a lid and let stand for 15 minutes. After 15 minutes, remove kombu pieces and add bonito flakes or dashi powder
  2. If using Instant Pot, press saute mode and bring water to a boil and then turn off saute mode. Add kombu pieces. Cover with a lid and let stand for 15 minutes. After 15 minutes, remove kombu pieces and add bonito flakes or dashi powder
  3. Add konnyaku pieces, fish cakes pieces, quail eggs and let them simmer for another 10 minutes and add the fried beancurd rolls the last and turn off the heat. They will soften by residual heat. If using Instant Pot, press saute mode again and simmer for another 10 minutes then add the fried beancurd rolls the last. Turn off saute mode
  4. Have a taste and adjust the seasonings to your taste and serve
